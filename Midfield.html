Midfield

Midfield is another very important position because of the fact that it connects the defense and the offense. Furthermore, they have to be physically fit due to the fact that they are constantly on the move to get from one end of the field to the other. Here are other tips for learning how to play Midfield. (The picture above is a picture of Iniesta. Go to the player's tab to find out more!)

  1. Midfield refers to the position between their team's defenders and fowards.
  2. Midfielders do a lot of the work and their main goal is to connect the defense to the offense.
  3. Midfielders can be split up into defensive midfielders and attacking midfielders. They are play on slightly different parts of the field.
  4. In a 4-3-3 formation, there is a left midfielder, center midfielder, and right midfielder. Player in these positions are responisble for helping the fowards when attacking, and steping back when the team is playing defensively.
  5. These players must be able to play in tight spaces.
  6. They also need to be fit and have endurance because they are constantly moving.
